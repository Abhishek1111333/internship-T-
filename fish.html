Fishes

Having an aquarium full of freshwater fish at home is a thing of beauty. Keeping an aquarium in your home adds numerous benefits to your health, like reducing stress and lowering blood pressure. Freshwater tanks tend to be easier to be maintained than saltwater tanks, which is why they are more popular. When choosing the best aquarium fish, it is important to consider how large your tank is and what other species you have lived there.

Gold Fish

Goldfish are probably the most popular freshwater fish for aquariums. These fish need constant maintenance, such as weekly water changes and filters. Goldfish can come in various shapes, sizes, colors, and breeds. Lifespan: 10-15 years

Guppies

Guppies are is one of the world’s most widely available aquarium fish. They are peaceful, easy-going community fish and mix well with other non-aggressive fish species. Lifespan: 3-4 years.

Molly

Molly fish have been popular among aquarium enthusiasts for many generations. These fish are known for their low-maintenance care requirements and wide selection of possible species to choose from. Lifespan: 2-5 years.

9 mins

Discus

Discus is one of the most famous exotic freshwater aquarium fish. Because of its wide gamut of color and intricate markings, the discus is one of the most popular tropical aquarium fish in the world. Lifespan: 10-15 years.

9 mins

Neon Tetras

Neon Tetras are neat and enjoyable freshwater fish that can be found in the tanks of hobbyists all over the world. They’re very attractive, playful, easy to be taken care of, and peaceful. Lifespan: 3-4 years.

9 mins

Cory Catfish

The Cory catfish is one of the most recommended freshwater fishes for all experience levels. They look awesome, they’re easy to care for, and they are super friendly. Looking for a peaceful beginner-friendly fish with tons of personality? Then cory catfish are best for you. Lifespan: 4-5 years.

9 mins

KilliFish

Killifish are not well known in the mainstream aquarium hobby and are seldom seen in local fish stores, but they are some of the most colorful freshwater aquarium fish in existence. Lifespan: 2-5 years.

9 mins

AngelFish

Possibly the most renowned freshwater fish in the aquarium hobby, angelfish belong to the family Cichlidae. Famous for their majestic swimming style, angelfish make gorgeous additions to large aquariums. Lifespan: 10-12 years.
